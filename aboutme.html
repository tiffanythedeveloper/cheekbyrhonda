BIO

I am Rhonda Collins, an Experienced Professional Makeup Artist who is trained and certified. I have been very blessed to have worked for numerous lines including a prestigious cosmetic company. I have had the pleasure of experiencing customer service as well as gaining knowledgeable information on various styles of makeup and makeup trends. I take pride and honor in satisfying my clients and making sure your needs are met. It is my personal duty as an artist to collaborate with you so we can bring your beautiful vision to Reality!
